Crazy Racing : Cops Smasher

You are wanted! Cops are coming for you! Take your driving to escape from cops, though the city.

  • Don't let them catch you. You can lead them to crash on obstacle
  • Your car is invulnerable to crash
  • Find power-ups in the city
  • Random generated environment
  • 60+ unlockable vehicles
  • See if you can beat your friends highscore in the online leaderboards!
